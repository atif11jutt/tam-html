Brian served Forsyth County as a Commissioner from 2004-16. He led the way on three different tax cuts, brought The Collection and Halcyon to South Forsyth, and was instrumental in the creation of Chattahoochee Pointe, Old Atlanta Park, Fowler Park, and Caney Creek Preserve through the 2008 parks bond.

Now he is running to return to the Board, because we need an experienced leader who isn’t afraid to stand against government waste and unwelcome development.

He’ll cut taxes for residents and homeowners again, stand against new apartments and highdensity, and put a stop to out-of-control spending.

Let’s restore accountability in Forsyth County.

On May 21, vote Brian Tam for County Commission.
